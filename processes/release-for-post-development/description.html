Release for Post-Development

Clause: 6.4.9

Objective

Confirm that the item is ready for production and post-development phases from a cybersecurity perspective.

Inputs

  • Cybersecurity case
  • Cybersecurity assessment report
  • Validation results

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-06-20: Cybersecurity release report
  • WP-06-21: Post-development cybersecurity requirements

Requirements

  • [RQ-06-33] Release for post-development shall be confirmed
  • [RQ-06-34] All cybersecurity activities shall be completed or planned
  • [RQ-06-35] Residual risks shall be documented and accepted
  • [RQ-06-36] Requirements for post-development shall be defined

Key Activities

  • Verify completion of all development phase activities
  • Confirm cybersecurity goals are achieved
  • Document known vulnerabilities and residual risks
  • Define requirements for production phase
  • Define requirements for operations and maintenance
  • Obtain formal release approval

Considerations

  • Production control requirements
  • Monitoring and incident response capabilities
  • Update and patch management processes
  • End-of-life and decommissioning plans
