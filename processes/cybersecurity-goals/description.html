Cybersecurity Goals

Clause: 9.4

Objective

Define high-level cybersecurity goals based on threat analysis and risk assessment to protect identified assets.

Inputs

  • Item definition
  • Asset identification
  • Threat scenarios
  • Risk values

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-09-04: Cybersecurity goals
  • WP-09-05: Goal allocation to components

Requirements

  • [RQ-09-05] Cybersecurity goals shall be defined based on TARA
  • [RQ-09-06] Goals shall address identified risks
  • [RQ-09-07] Goals shall be allocated to components
  • [RQ-09-08] CAL (Cybersecurity Assurance Level) shall be assigned

Key Activities

  • Derive cybersecurity goals from risk treatment decisions
  • Define goals addressing CIA (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability)
  • Assign CAL to each goal based on risk
  • Allocate goals to item components
  • Establish traceability to threat scenarios
  • Review goals with stakeholders

Cybersecurity Assurance Levels (CAL)

  • CAL 1: Low assurance, basic measures
  • CAL 2: Moderate assurance, standard measures
  • CAL 3: High assurance, rigorous measures
  • CAL 4: Highest assurance, comprehensive measures
