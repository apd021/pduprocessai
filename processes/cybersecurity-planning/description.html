Cybersecurity Planning

Clause: 6.4.2

Objective

Plan cybersecurity activities for the project to ensure systematic implementation of cybersecurity throughout the development lifecycle.

Inputs

  • Item definition
  • Cybersecurity responsibilities
  • Organizational processes
  • Project schedule

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-06-03: Cybersecurity plan
  • WP-06-04: Cybersecurity milestone schedule

Requirements

  • [RQ-06-05] A cybersecurity plan shall be created for the project
  • [RQ-06-06] The plan shall define cybersecurity activities and milestones
  • [RQ-06-07] Dependencies and interfaces shall be identified
  • [RQ-06-08] Resources shall be planned for cybersecurity activities

Key Activities

  • Define cybersecurity activities for each development phase
  • Establish cybersecurity milestones aligned with project gates
  • Plan threat analysis and risk assessment activities
  • Schedule cybersecurity reviews and audits
  • Define verification and validation activities
  • Plan post-development cybersecurity activities
