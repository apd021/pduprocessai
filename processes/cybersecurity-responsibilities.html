Cybersecurity Responsibilities

Clause: 6.4.1

Objective

Define and assign cybersecurity responsibilities for the project to ensure accountability and proper execution of cybersecurity activities.

Inputs

  • Project definition
  • Organizational cybersecurity policy
  • Customer requirements

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-06-01: Cybersecurity responsibility assignment
  • WP-06-02: Project organizational chart

Requirements

  • [RQ-06-01] Cybersecurity responsibilities shall be assigned for the project
  • [RQ-06-02] A person responsible for cybersecurity shall be designated
  • [RQ-06-03] Responsibilities shall cover all project phases
  • [RQ-06-04] Interface responsibilities shall be clearly defined

Key Activities

  • Designate a Cybersecurity Manager for the project
  • Define roles for cybersecurity engineering, testing, and assessment
  • Establish responsibility matrix (RACI)
  • Define escalation paths for cybersecurity decisions
  • Document interfaces with suppliers and customers
  • Ensure competence of assigned personnel
