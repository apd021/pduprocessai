Item Definition

Clause: 9.3

Objective

Define the item and its operational environment as the basis for cybersecurity engineering activities.

Inputs

  • Customer requirements
  • Product concept and specifications
  • Regulatory requirements

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-09-01: Item definition
  • WP-09-02: Operational environment description
  • WP-09-03: Preliminary architecture

Requirements

  • [RQ-09-01] The item shall be defined with its boundary
  • [RQ-09-02] The operational environment shall be described
  • [RQ-09-03] Functions and interfaces shall be identified
  • [RQ-09-04] Preliminary architecture shall be documented

Key Activities

  • Define item boundary and scope
  • Describe operational context and use cases
  • Identify item functions and capabilities
  • Document external interfaces and connections
  • Describe preliminary architecture
  • Identify related items and dependencies

Content of Item Definition

  • Item boundary and components
  • Functions and services provided
  • External interfaces (in-vehicle, V2X, backend)
  • Operational modes and states
  • User interactions
  • Assumptions and constraints
