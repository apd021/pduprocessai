Cybersecurity Governance

Clause: 5.4.1

Objective

Establish and maintain cybersecurity policies, rules, and processes to manage cybersecurity risks throughout the organization.

Inputs

  • Organizational context and scope
  • Regulatory requirements and standards
  • Business objectives and risk appetite

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-05-01: Cybersecurity policy
  • WP-05-02: Organizational rules and processes for cybersecurity
  • WP-05-03: Evidence of competence, awareness and continuous improvement

Requirements

  • [RQ-05-01] A cybersecurity policy shall be defined and maintained
  • [RQ-05-02] Organizational rules and processes shall be established to manage cybersecurity
  • [RQ-05-03] Responsibilities shall be assigned for cybersecurity activities
  • [RQ-05-04] Resources shall be provided for cybersecurity implementation
  • [RQ-05-05] Management commitment to cybersecurity shall be demonstrated

Key Activities

  • Define cybersecurity policy aligned with organizational objectives
  • Establish governance structure with clear roles and responsibilities
  • Define processes for risk assessment and treatment
  • Implement continuous monitoring and improvement mechanisms
  • Ensure compliance with applicable regulations (e.g., UNECE WP.29)
