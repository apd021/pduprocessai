Cybersecurity Monitoring

Clause: 7.4.1

Objective

Continuously monitor for cybersecurity information that may affect the organization's products to enable timely detection and response.

Inputs

  • Information sharing sources
  • Vulnerability databases
  • Threat intelligence feeds
  • Product configurations and components

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-07-01: Cybersecurity monitoring plan
  • WP-07-02: Monitored information records
  • WP-07-03: Detected events log

Requirements

  • [RQ-07-01] Cybersecurity monitoring shall be performed continuously
  • [RQ-07-02] Relevant information sources shall be monitored
  • [RQ-07-03] Detected events shall be recorded and triaged
  • [RQ-07-04] Trigger criteria for event evaluation shall be defined

Key Activities

  • Define monitoring scope and sources
  • Monitor vulnerability databases (CVE, NVD, vendor advisories)
  • Track security research and disclosure activities
  • Monitor field incidents and customer reports
  • Subscribe to relevant threat intelligence services
  • Maintain Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) for correlation
