Cybersecurity Case

Clause: 6.4.7

Objective

Create and maintain a structured argument that demonstrates cybersecurity of the item or component throughout its lifecycle.

Inputs

  • All cybersecurity work products
  • Threat analysis and risk assessment results
  • Verification and validation results

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-06-16: Cybersecurity case
  • WP-06-17: Cybersecurity claims and arguments

Requirements

  • [RQ-06-25] A cybersecurity case shall be created for the item
  • [RQ-06-26] The case shall provide argument for cybersecurity achievement
  • [RQ-06-27] The case shall reference supporting evidence
  • [RQ-06-28] The case shall be maintained throughout the lifecycle

Key Activities

  • Define cybersecurity claims and objectives
  • Develop structured arguments supporting claims
  • Link arguments to supporting evidence (work products)
  • Document residual risks and their acceptance
  • Maintain traceability throughout all evidence
  • Update the case when changes occur

Structure

The cybersecurity case typically includes:

  • Cybersecurity claim(s)
  • Arguments supporting each claim
  • Evidence (referenced work products)
  • Assumptions and context
