Organizational Cybersecurity Audit

Clause: 5.4.7

Objective

Conduct audits to verify that organizational cybersecurity management is implemented effectively and complies with policies and standards.

Inputs

  • Cybersecurity policy
  • Organizational rules and processes
  • Previous audit results

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-05-16: Cybersecurity audit plan
  • WP-05-17: Cybersecurity audit report
  • WP-05-18: Corrective action records

Requirements

  • [RQ-05-22] Organizational cybersecurity audits shall be performed
  • [RQ-05-23] Audits shall verify compliance with cybersecurity policies and processes
  • [RQ-05-24] Audit findings shall be addressed through corrective actions
  • [RQ-05-25] Audit results shall be used to improve cybersecurity management

Key Activities

  • Define audit scope, criteria, and schedule
  • Select and train qualified auditors
  • Conduct internal and external audits
  • Document audit findings and non-conformities
  • Track and verify corrective actions
  • Report audit results to management
