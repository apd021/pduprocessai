Threat Scenario Identification

Clause: 15.4

Objective

Identify potential threat scenarios that could lead to damage through compromise of identified assets.

Inputs

  • Asset identification
  • Damage scenarios
  • Threat intelligence

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-15-04: Threat scenario catalog
  • WP-15-05: Threat actor analysis

Requirements

  • [RQ-15-04] Threat scenarios shall be identified for assets
  • [RQ-15-05] Threat actors and motivations shall be considered
  • [RQ-15-06] Threat scenarios shall link to damage scenarios

Key Activities

  • Identify potential threat actors
  • Define threat actor capabilities and motivations
  • Develop threat scenarios for each asset
  • Use threat modeling techniques (STRIDE, Attack Trees)
  • Consider automotive-specific threats
  • Document threat scenarios with traceability

Threat Actors

  • Script kiddies: Low skill, opportunistic
  • Hacktivists: Ideologically motivated
  • Criminals: Financially motivated
  • Competitors: Business advantage
  • Nation states: High capability, strategic goals
  • Insiders: Authorized access, various motivations

STRIDE Categories

  • Spoofing identity
  • Tampering with data
  • Repudiation
  • Information disclosure
  • Denial of service
  • Elevation of privilege
