Impact Rating

Clause: 15.5

Objective

Assess the potential impact of damage scenarios on road users, other parties, and the organization.

Inputs

  • Damage scenarios
  • Threat scenarios
  • Regulatory requirements

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-15-06: Impact rating results
  • WP-15-07: Impact analysis documentation

Requirements

  • [RQ-15-07] Impact shall be rated for each damage scenario
  • [RQ-15-08] Multiple impact categories shall be considered
  • [RQ-15-09] Impact ratings shall be justified

Key Activities

  • Assess safety impact on road users
  • Evaluate financial impact
  • Assess operational impact
  • Evaluate privacy and regulatory impact
  • Determine overall impact rating
  • Document rationale for ratings

Impact Categories

  • Safety (S): Physical harm to persons
  • Financial (F): Monetary losses
  • Operational (O): Service disruption
  • Privacy (P): Personal data exposure

Impact Levels
