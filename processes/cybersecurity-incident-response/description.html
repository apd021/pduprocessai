Cybersecurity Incident Response

Clause: 13.4.1

Objective

Respond to cybersecurity incidents affecting products in the field to minimize impact and restore security.

Inputs

  • Incident reports
  • Vulnerability analysis results
  • Monitoring data

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-13-01: Incident response plan
  • WP-13-02: Incident records
  • WP-13-03: Incident response report

Requirements

  • [RQ-13-01] Incident response plan shall be established
  • [RQ-13-02] Incidents shall be detected and reported
  • [RQ-13-03] Response actions shall be executed promptly
  • [RQ-13-04] Lessons learned shall be documented

Key Activities

  • Establish incident response team and procedures
  • Define incident classification and escalation
  • Investigate and contain incidents
  • Coordinate with stakeholders and authorities
  • Implement remediation measures
  • Conduct post-incident review

Incident Response Phases

  • Preparation: Plans, teams, tools ready
  • Detection: Identify potential incidents
  • Analysis: Investigate and assess impact
  • Containment: Limit damage spread
  • Eradication: Remove threat
  • Recovery: Restore normal operation
  • Lessons learned: Improve processes
