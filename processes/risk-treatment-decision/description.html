Risk Treatment Decision

Clause: 15.9

Objective

Make decisions on how to treat identified cybersecurity risks based on risk values and organizational risk acceptance criteria.

Inputs

  • Risk values
  • Risk acceptance criteria
  • Treatment options

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-15-15: Risk treatment decisions
  • WP-15-16: Cybersecurity goals (derived from treatment)
  • WP-15-17: Residual risk documentation

Requirements

  • [RQ-15-19] Treatment decision shall be made for each risk
  • [RQ-15-20] Treatment options shall be evaluated
  • [RQ-15-21] Residual risk shall be assessed and documented
  • [RQ-15-22] Cybersecurity goals shall be derived from treatment

Key Activities

  • Evaluate treatment options for each risk
  • Select appropriate treatment strategy
  • Define cybersecurity goals and requirements
  • Assess residual risk after treatment
  • Obtain approval for risk acceptance
  • Document treatment decisions

Treatment Options

  • Avoid: Eliminate the risk source
  • Reduce: Implement controls to lower risk
  • Share: Transfer risk to another party
  • Retain: Accept the risk with justification

Decision Criteria

  • Risk value vs. acceptance threshold
  • Cost-benefit of treatment options
  • Technical feasibility
  • Regulatory requirements
  • Customer expectations
  • Organizational risk appetite

Residual Risk

After treatment, residual risk shall be:

  • Reassessed using the same methodology
  • Documented with justification
  • Approved by appropriate authority
  • Monitored throughout the lifecycle
