Business Translation: Updates

Business Value

Implement updates to ensure cybersecurity compliance, reduce risks, and meet customer and regulatory requirements.

Risk Reduction

  • Compliance: Meet ISO/SAE 21434 and UNECE WP.29 R155 requirements
  • Quality: Reduce security vulnerabilities in products
  • Reputation: Protect brand value through demonstrated security practices

Stakeholder Impact

  • Customers: Increased confidence in product security
  • Regulators: Demonstrated compliance with cybersecurity regulations
  • Engineering Teams: Clear processes and expectations
  • Management: Visibility into cybersecurity posture

Cost Considerations

  • Implementation: Initial setup and process definition
  • Ongoing: Regular execution and maintenance
  • ROI: Reduced rework, faster certification, avoided incidents
