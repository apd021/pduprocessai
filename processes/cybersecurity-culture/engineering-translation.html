Engineering Translation: Cybersecurity Culture

Technical Implementation

Build technical competence and awareness among engineering teams through structured training programs and knowledge management systems.

Implementation Approach

  • Competence Framework: Define required skills matrix for cybersecurity roles (TARA analysts, security architects, testers)
  • Training Curriculum: Develop technical training modules covering threat modeling, secure coding, vulnerability analysis
  • Knowledge Base: Create internal wiki with security patterns, attack vectors, and mitigation strategies
  • Mentoring Program: Pair junior engineers with security experts for hands-on learning
  • Certification Path: Support engineers in obtaining security certifications (CISSP, CEH, etc.)

Technical Specifications

  • Competence requirements matrix by role
  • Training course syllabi and materials
  • Knowledge base structure and content
  • Competence assessment criteria
  • Training delivery platform (LMS)

Engineering Artifacts

  • WP-05-04: Training records and certificates
  • WP-05-05: Competence management database
  • Training materials and presentations
  • Knowledge base articles
  • Competence gap analysis reports

Tools and Methods

  • Learning Management System (LMS)
  • Knowledge management platform (Confluence, SharePoint)
  • Competence tracking tools
  • Security training platforms (Cybrary, SANS)
  • Internal security labs for hands-on practice
