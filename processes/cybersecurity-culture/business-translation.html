Business Translation: Cybersecurity Culture

Business Value

Develop organizational capability to consistently apply cybersecurity practices, reducing human error and improving security outcomes.

Risk Reduction

  • Human Error Mitigation: Reduce security vulnerabilities introduced through lack of awareness
  • Early Detection: Enable engineers to identify security issues during development
  • Consistent Quality: Ensure uniform application of security practices across projects

Stakeholder Impact

  • Engineering Teams: Increased confidence and competence in security work
  • Project Managers: Reduced security-related delays and rework
  • Customers: Higher quality deliverables with fewer security issues
  • HR: Improved employee retention through professional development

Cost Considerations

  • Training Investment: Initial training costs (~€5K-20K per engineer)
  • Ongoing Costs: Continuous learning and certification maintenance
  • ROI: Reduced security defects, faster development cycles, lower incident response costs
