Business Translation: Asset Identification

Business Value

Establish foundation for risk assessment by identifying what needs protection, enabling targeted security investments.

Risk Reduction

  • Targeted Protection: Focus security measures on assets that matter most
  • Risk Prioritization: Enable informed decisions on security investments
  • Compliance: Demonstrate systematic approach to asset protection

Stakeholder Impact

  • Security Architects: Clear understanding of protection targets
  • Management: Visibility into security-critical assets
  • Customers: Confidence in systematic security approach

Cost Considerations

  • Analysis Effort: 1-2 weeks per project phase
  • ROI: Prevents over-engineering and under-protection
