Cybersecurity Culture

Clause: 5.4.2

Objective

Create and maintain a cybersecurity-aware culture within the organization to support effective cybersecurity implementation.

Inputs

  • Cybersecurity policy
  • Organizational structure
  • Competence requirements

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-05-04: Evidence of cybersecurity culture (training records, awareness programs)
  • WP-05-05: Competence management records

Requirements

  • [RQ-05-06] A cybersecurity culture shall be promoted within the organization
  • [RQ-05-07] Management shall support the creation and maintenance of a cybersecurity culture
  • [RQ-05-08] Continuous improvement of cybersecurity awareness shall be implemented

Key Activities

  • Develop and deliver cybersecurity awareness training programs
  • Define competence requirements for personnel involved in cybersecurity
  • Maintain training and competence records
  • Promote cybersecurity best practices across the organization
  • Conduct regular security awareness campaigns
  • Establish channels for reporting security concerns
