Management Systems

Clause: 5.4.4

Objective

Integrate cybersecurity management into existing organizational management systems to ensure consistent and effective implementation.

Inputs

  • Existing management systems (QMS, ISMS)
  • Cybersecurity policy
  • Organizational processes

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-05-08: Integrated management system documentation
  • WP-05-09: Process interface descriptions

Requirements

  • [RQ-05-12] Cybersecurity shall be integrated into existing management systems
  • [RQ-05-13] Interfaces between management systems shall be defined
  • [RQ-05-14] Consistency between cybersecurity and other management systems shall be ensured

Key Activities

  • Align cybersecurity management with quality management (IATF 16949)
  • Integrate with functional safety management (ISO 26262)
  • Interface with information security management (ISO 27001)
  • Define common processes and documentation
  • Establish configuration and change management integration
  • Coordinate audit and review activities
