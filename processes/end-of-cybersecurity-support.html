End of Cybersecurity Support

Clause: 14.4.1

Objective

Plan and communicate the end of cybersecurity support for products, ensuring stakeholders are informed and risks are managed.

Inputs

  • Product lifecycle planning
  • Support agreements
  • Customer requirements

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-14-01: End of cybersecurity support plan
  • WP-14-02: Stakeholder communication
  • WP-14-03: Risk assessment for end of support

Requirements

  • [RQ-14-01] End of cybersecurity support shall be planned
  • [RQ-14-02] Stakeholders shall be informed in advance
  • [RQ-14-03] Risks shall be assessed and communicated
  • [RQ-14-04] Transition procedures shall be defined

Key Activities

  • Define end-of-support timeline
  • Assess risks of continued operation without support
  • Communicate with customers and stakeholders
  • Provide guidance for continued safe operation
  • Define handover procedures if applicable
  • Archive relevant documentation

Considerations

  • Regulatory requirements for support duration
  • Customer expectations and contracts
  • Availability of spare parts and updates
  • Migration or upgrade paths
  • Documentation preservation
