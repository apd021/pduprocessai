Tailoring

Clause: 6.4.3

Objective

Tailor organizational cybersecurity processes to the specific needs of the project based on risk assessment and justification.

Inputs

  • Organizational cybersecurity processes
  • Item definition
  • Project risk assessment

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-06-05: Tailoring rationale
  • WP-06-06: Tailored process documentation

Requirements

  • [RQ-06-09] Tailoring of cybersecurity activities shall be justified
  • [RQ-06-10] Tailoring rationale shall be documented
  • [RQ-06-11] Tailoring shall not reduce the achieved cybersecurity level
  • [RQ-06-12] Tailored processes shall be approved by relevant stakeholders

Key Activities

  • Identify opportunities for process tailoring
  • Assess impact of tailoring on cybersecurity
  • Document rationale for each tailoring decision
  • Obtain approval for tailored processes
  • Maintain traceability to original requirements
  • Review tailoring decisions periodically
