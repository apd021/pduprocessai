Business Translation: Design

Business Value

Build security into products from the start, reducing vulnerabilities and avoiding costly rework later in development.

Risk Reduction

  • Early Security: Address security during design phase when changes are cheaper
  • Vulnerability Prevention: Prevent security flaws through secure design practices
  • Cost Efficiency: Avoid expensive security fixes in later phases

Stakeholder Impact

  • Development Teams: Clear security requirements and guidelines
  • Product Quality: Higher quality products with fewer security issues
  • Customers: More secure products, reduced risk of incidents

Cost Considerations

  • Design Phase: Additional security design effort (~10-15% overhead)
  • ROI: 10x cost savings vs. fixing issues in production
  • Avoided Costs: Reduced vulnerabilities, faster certification, fewer recalls
