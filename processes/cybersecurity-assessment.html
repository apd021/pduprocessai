Cybersecurity Assessment

Clause: 6.4.8

Objective

Independently assess whether the cybersecurity activities have been properly performed and the cybersecurity goals have been achieved.

Inputs

  • Cybersecurity case
  • Cybersecurity work products
  • Verification and validation results

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-06-18: Cybersecurity assessment report
  • WP-06-19: Assessment findings and recommendations

Requirements

  • [RQ-06-29] Cybersecurity assessment shall be performed
  • [RQ-06-30] Assessment shall verify achievement of cybersecurity goals
  • [RQ-06-31] Assessors shall be independent from development
  • [RQ-06-32] Assessment results shall be documented

Key Activities

  • Review cybersecurity case and supporting evidence
  • Verify completeness of cybersecurity activities
  • Assess adequacy of TARA and risk treatment
  • Review verification and validation coverage
  • Identify weaknesses and improvement opportunities
  • Provide recommendation for release

Independence Levels

  • I0: Self-assessment (no independence)
  • I1: Independent person within same organization
  • I2: Independent organizational unit
  • I3: External independent organization
