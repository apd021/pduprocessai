Information Sharing

Clause: 5.4.3

Objective

Establish processes for sharing cybersecurity information with relevant stakeholders to support vulnerability management and incident response.

Inputs

  • Cybersecurity monitoring data
  • Vulnerability information
  • Incident reports
  • Threat intelligence

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-05-06: Information sharing agreements
  • WP-05-07: Shared cybersecurity information records

Requirements

  • [RQ-05-09] Processes shall be established to share cybersecurity information
  • [RQ-05-10] Relevant sources for cybersecurity information shall be identified
  • [RQ-05-11] Cybersecurity information shall be shared in a timely manner

Key Activities

  • Identify information sharing partners (OEMs, suppliers, ISACs, CERTs)
  • Establish information sharing agreements and protocols
  • Define classification levels for shared information
  • Participate in industry information sharing initiatives (Auto-ISAC)
  • Monitor external cybersecurity information sources
  • Share vulnerability and threat information with relevant parties
