Request for Quotation

Clause: 8.4.2

Objective

Define and communicate cybersecurity requirements to suppliers during the quotation process.

Inputs

  • Project cybersecurity requirements
  • Supplier capability requirements
  • Technical specifications

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-08-04: Cybersecurity requirements in RFQ
  • WP-08-05: Supplier cybersecurity questionnaire
  • WP-08-06: RFQ evaluation criteria

Requirements

  • [RQ-08-05] Cybersecurity requirements shall be included in RFQ
  • [RQ-08-06] Expected deliverables shall be specified
  • [RQ-08-07] Supplier responsibilities shall be communicated
  • [RQ-08-08] Evaluation criteria shall include cybersecurity

Key Activities

  • Define cybersecurity requirements for the component/service
  • Specify required work products and evidence
  • Define expected compliance with standards (ISO 21434)
  • Include vulnerability disclosure requirements
  • Specify post-development support requirements
  • Evaluate supplier responses against criteria

RFQ Content

  • Cybersecurity requirements and specifications
  • Required work products and deliverables
  • Compliance and certification requirements
  • Responsibility allocation expectations
  • Support and maintenance requirements
