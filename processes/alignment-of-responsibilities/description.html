Alignment of Responsibilities

Clause: 8.4.3

Objective

Establish clear alignment of cybersecurity responsibilities between customer and supplier through formal agreements.

Inputs

  • RFQ responses
  • Project cybersecurity requirements
  • Supplier capability assessment

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-08-07: Cybersecurity Interface Agreement (CIA)
  • WP-08-08: Responsibility matrix
  • WP-08-09: Interface specifications

Requirements

  • [RQ-08-09] Responsibilities shall be agreed between parties
  • [RQ-08-10] Agreement shall cover all cybersecurity activities
  • [RQ-08-11] Interfaces and communication shall be defined
  • [RQ-08-12] Agreement shall address post-development activities

Key Activities

  • Negotiate and agree on responsibility distribution
  • Define work product deliverables and acceptance criteria
  • Establish communication and escalation channels
  • Define vulnerability disclosure and response processes
  • Agree on audit and assessment rights
  • Document agreements in CIA or contract

Agreement Topics

  • Development phase responsibilities
  • Verification and validation responsibilities
  • Work product ownership and handover
  • Vulnerability management and disclosure
  • Incident response coordination
  • End-of-life and transition planning
