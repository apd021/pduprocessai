Risk Value Determination

Clause: 15.8

Objective

Determine the cybersecurity risk value by combining impact rating and attack feasibility for each threat scenario.

Inputs

  • Impact ratings
  • Attack feasibility ratings
  • Risk acceptance criteria

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-15-12: Risk values
  • WP-15-13: Risk matrix
  • WP-15-14: Risk register

Requirements

  • [RQ-15-16] Risk value shall be determined for each threat scenario
  • [RQ-15-17] Risk determination method shall be defined
  • [RQ-15-18] Risk values shall support treatment decisions

Key Activities

  • Define risk determination methodology
  • Combine impact and feasibility ratings
  • Apply risk matrix or formula
  • Categorize risks by level
  • Prioritize risks for treatment
  • Document risk assessment results

Risk Matrix

Impact ↓ / Feasibility → High Medium Low Very Low
Severe 5 4 3 2
Major 4 3 2 1
Moderate 3 2 1 1
Negligible 2 1 1 1

Risk Levels

  • 5 - Critical: Immediate treatment required
  • 4 - High: Treatment required
  • 3 - Medium: Treatment recommended
  • 2 - Low: Treatment considered
  • 1 - Negligible: May be accepted
