Supplier Capability

Clause: 8.4.1

Objective

Assess and manage supplier capability to perform cybersecurity activities as required for the project.

Inputs

  • Supplier qualification requirements
  • Project cybersecurity requirements
  • Supplier information and certifications

Outputs / Work Products

  • WP-08-01: Supplier cybersecurity capability assessment
  • WP-08-02: Approved supplier list
  • WP-08-03: Capability gap analysis

Requirements

  • [RQ-08-01] Supplier cybersecurity capability shall be assessed
  • [RQ-08-02] Assessment criteria shall be defined
  • [RQ-08-03] Capability gaps shall be identified and addressed
  • [RQ-08-04] Supplier selection shall consider cybersecurity capability

Key Activities

  • Define cybersecurity capability requirements for suppliers
  • Assess supplier processes and certifications (e.g., ISO 21434, ISO 27001)
  • Evaluate supplier track record and references
  • Identify capability gaps and required improvements
  • Define conditions for supplier approval
  • Periodically reassess supplier capability

Assessment Criteria

  • Cybersecurity management processes
  • Competence and training
  • Security development practices
  • Vulnerability management capability
  • Incident response readiness
